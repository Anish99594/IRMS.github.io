About Us

A smart library is a physical site that provide online access to digitalized audio, video, and written material. It provides free copies of books available to the users. Students can search by topic to retrieve a manageable amount of quality content, quickly and easily. The information available on the web is not uniformly distributed and only a small portion of which exists in print. Unlike other website our website consists of all the related books in one place and their relevant videos so that the users can gain much knowledge.

Our Mission

For creating our website the technologies we have used are HTML, CSS, JAVA-SCRIPT and BOOTSTRAP. HTML stands for hypertext markup language . With HTML you can create your own Website. CSS is the language used to style a website. Java script is used for getting the behaviour of our web pages. We have used google font-awesome for getting the icons related to the website. Our website is for students and their convenience in getting the books of their relevant subject .
